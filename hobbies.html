Chicago Sports

I am from the Chicago area and grew up watching Chicago sports. I am a massive sports fan in general, but the Bears, Cubs, Bulls, and Blackhawks are my favorite teams. My favorite team out of all of these is probably the Cubs. Second would be the Bulls. The Bears are dead to me since they don't know how to run a competent football team and always leave me heartbroken. But I still love going to all games in Chicago no matter the team (besides the White Sox). There's just something about the atmosphere of being a Chicago sports fan. Especially a Cubs fan.

Running/Exercise

I love to be active, whether it's running, lifting, or playing sports. I did cross country in high school, hence the photo. The photo was actually taken at my senior year conference meet, my final race in high school. Yes, that is all mud, and yes it was miserable to run in, but probably the most rewarding race as well. The thing I love about running is that is hard and it challenges you a lot both physically and mentally. When I hear people complain about running I usually laugh, because I know that if they really pushed themselves and cared they would love it. I love playing other sports as well, like football and basketball. Running is a pretty independent sport, so being able to accomplish things with a team is a different type of reward.
